About

BETWEEN
WORLDS.

I'm Pola Rasmy — an Egyptian director of cinematic brand, automotive and destination films. I lead full productions and international crews under my own studio, RASMY, between Cairo and China.

I've shot for Mercedes-Benz, Ferrari and HAVAL, and I'm credited in the camera department on Apple TV+'s Fountain of Youth.

My work moves both ways: Chinese craft and industry filmed for the MENA market — and the region's stories carried back.

Officially invited by Chinese authorities as a China Tourism Ambassador — Beijing · Guangzhou · Quanzhou.

Stories travel.
I build the ones that stay.
